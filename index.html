Welcome to the Biomedical Research Interest Club

Our Mission

The Biomedical Research Interest Club (BRIC) at the University of Alberta Mission: BRIC is dedicated to fostering a vibrant undergraduate research community in the biomedical sciences. We aim to ignite passion for research, enhance accessibility to opportunities, and showcase student achievements in this exciting field.

Our Goals:

  1. Increase interest in biomedical research among undergraduates.
  2. Improve access to research opportunities and resources.
  3. Highlight student contributions to the University of Alberta's research community.
